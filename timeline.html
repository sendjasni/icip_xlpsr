Challenge Timeline

Key dates and milestones for the XLPSR Challenge at IEEE ICIP 2026

Follow the progress from launch to winner announcement

Important Registration Note

All participants must complete the official registration form before accessing the dataset and submission platform. The timeline below outlines the challenge phases and key deadlines.

Feb 11, 2026 Current Phase

Official Challenge Launch

Challenge documentation, rules, and registration form go live. Participants can begin the registration process to access the dataset and submission platform.

Feb 11, 2026

IMPROVED Public Validation Set Release

The validation dataset becomes available to registered participants through CodaBench. This set is used for development and preliminary testing of solutions.

May 13, 2026

Final Submission Deadline

Deadline for submitting final solutions and the mandatory two-page extended abstract describing the approach. All submissions must be made through the CodaBench platform.

May 14 – May 31, 2026

Internal Evaluation Period

The organizing team evaluates all submissions using the automated screening protocol (OCR + VQM metrics) followed by expert panel assessment.

June 10, 2026

Extended Abstract Acceptance Notification

Authors are notified about the acceptance of their two-page extended abstracts for inclusion in the ICIP 2026 Grand Challenge proceedings.

July 1, 2026

Camera-ready Abstract Submission

Deadline for submitting the final camera-ready version of the two-page extended abstract, incorporating any requested changes from reviewers.

13–17 September 2026

ICIP 2026 Conference & Winner Announcement

Grand Challenge results and winners are announced during a special session at IEEE ICIP 2026 in Tampere, Finland. Winning teams will be recognized and invited to present their approaches.
